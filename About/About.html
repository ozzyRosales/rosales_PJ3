Our Mission

At its core, Dream Fest is a beacon of hope and support for DACA recipients, embodying the spirit of solidarity and community that defines California. The festivities serve as a rallying point, bringing together people from diverse backgrounds to stand in solidarity with Dreamers and advocate for their rights and inclusion.

Our Purpose

Each day of Dream Fest is packed with a rich tapestry of activities, performances, workshops, and discussions, all designed to foster connections, raise awareness, and build bridges of understanding. From educational seminars on immigration policy to artistic showcases celebrating the creativity and resilience of DACA recipients, every aspect of the event is meticulously curated to inspire, educate, and empower. Dream Fest stands as a beacon of support and celebration for dreamers, providing a platform where their voices are heard, their stories shared, and their contributions recognized.

Our Community

By taking place simultaneously in three major cities, Dream Fest transcends geographical boundaries, uniting communities across California in a shared commitment to support and uplift DACA recipients. The event serves as a powerful reminder of the state's unwavering dedication to inclusivity, diversity, and the pursuit of the American Dream for all who call California home.
