DAY 1

The festival kicks off with a vibrant cultural celebration, featuring performances by Latin artists, musicians, and dancers, highlighting the rich immigrant tapestry of America.


Day 2

The second day includes raffles with prizes such as school supplies, computers, and gift cards. With food and drinks offered throughout the event.


Day 3

The festival concludes with a panel discussion titled "Voices of DACA: Stories of Resilience and Hope," where DACA recipients from diverse sectors like education, law, business, and the arts.
